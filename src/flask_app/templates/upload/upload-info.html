Upload Instructions

Step 1: Image Upload
Click on the 'Choose file' button to select a file from your local storage. Please ensure that you select an image of a sudoku board that fits within the size requirement and that has enough clarity so that it can be interpreted by the program.
Step 2: Upload Confirmation
Once your file has been selected, you will be shown a preview of the image and, if the image is the one that you wish to upload, click the 'Submit' button. Otherwise, click on the 'Cancel' button or the 'X' in the top right corner in order to go back to selecting your image.
Step 3: Upload Verification
Once your submitted image has been processed, you will be shown the extracted sudoku board from the image as well as the digits extracted onto the web-app's board. From here, you can make any necessary edits to the cells of the board if there are any discrepencies between the extracted image and the loaded board. After you have confirmed all of your desired changes, if any, press the 'Solve' button to submit your board.
Step 4: View Solution
You will be taken to a screen that showcases the solved board with all the new numbers highlighted as green text. This way, you can distinguish between the original numbers of the board and the solution to the supplied board. Afterwards, you can choose to upload another board, or go ahead and use one of the other functions in our Sudoku Suite!
