Upload Instructions

Step 1: Image Upload
Click on the 'Choose file' button to select an image from your local storage or camera. The image must be at least 200 x 200 pixels.

For best results, ensure that the uploaded picture has adequate brightness / contrast, and features the Sudoku board as prominently as possible.
Step 2: Preview
A preview of the image will be displayed. Please ensure the clarity and orientation of the image, and then click 'Submit' to upload.
Step 3: Verification
Once the submitted image has been processed, the extracted sudoku board and its recognized digits will be displayed. You can make any necessary edits to the cells of the board if there exist any discrepencies on the processed board. Press the 'Solve' button to submit the puzzle for automatic solving.
Step 4: Solution
Voila! You can now view the solution, or if one is not available, click 'Back' to double check the puzzle to ensure that the puzzle is solvable.
